About Us - PCCW Solutions.

Lenovo PCCW Solutions is a leading technology solutions powerhouse in Asia Pacific. A strategic partnership formed between Lenovo and PCCW in August 2022, the company offers market-leading solutions to help organizations transform their operations, increase competitiveness, and enhance innovation through technology. Its wide range of technology solutions and service offerings include system integration, application development and operation, IT outsourcing, managed services, digital solutions, and technical services.

  • Founded in 2000.
  • Specialties in Cloud Computing, IT Out-source, Managed Services, and Digital & IoT.
  • More than 2000 employees.
